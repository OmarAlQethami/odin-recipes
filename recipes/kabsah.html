Kabsah

Kabsah is an Arab mixed rice dish, served on a communal platter, that originates from Saudi Arabia And it's commonly regarded as a national dish in all the countries of the Arabian Peninsula.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Meat
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Salsa
  • Spices

Steps:

  1. Boil the water and put the meat and salt and salsa and spices and leave it for 30 minutes
  2. Put the rice and wait until the water is gone
  3. Happy eating!
